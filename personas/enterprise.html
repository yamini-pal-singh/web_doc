Pilot to production path

Week 1–2 — Proof of concept

  • Spin up cloud API credentials (free $200 credit covers ~50 hours of audio).
  • Pick one use case with a clear metric (agent handle time, scribe time saved, conversion lift).
  • Benchmark on 50–200 of your own audio samples to validate accuracy in-domain.

Week 3–4 — Integration pilot

  • Wire into one production system (CRM, telephony, or EHR) for a 5–10% traffic slice.
  • Instrument end-to-end latency, WER drift, and failure modes.
  • Review with security: SOC 2 report, ISO certificate, BAA if needed.

Week 5+ — Scale out

  • Decide cloud vs on-prem based on pilot findings.
  • Negotiate enterprise agreement: committed volume, SLAs, support tier.
  • If on-prem: Triton containers deployed into your VPC or datacenter.
  • Route production traffic with canary → ramp.
