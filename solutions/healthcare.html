Common workflows Illustrative

Real-time consultation scribe

  • Microphone in the consultation room streams to Shunya ASR over WebSocket.
  • zero-med transcribes with clinical vocabulary; live transcript shows on the physician's screen for review and correction.
  • On visit close, the structuring layer maps the transcript to SOAP (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan) sections and emits FHIR resources: Encounter, Observation, MedicationRequest, Condition.
  • The physician reviews and signs. Data goes to the EHR.

Hospital call-centre triage

  • Patient calls the hospital hotline. Shunya identifies the caller's language automatically.
  • Intent detection routes: appointment_booking, test_result_enquiry, emergency, insurance.
  • Emergency intents trigger immediate human transfer with auto-transcribed symptom notes for the paramedic.

Post-surgery debrief capture

  • Surgeon dictates a debrief immediately after surgery (iPad or mic).
  • Batch transcription with zero-med; keyterm normalization against hospital formulary and procedure list.
  • Auto-generated operative note pre-populates the EHR for physician signoff.

HIPAA posture

BAA executed

Business Associate Agreement signed for covered entities. Shunya accepts PHI processing under standard HIPAA terms.

Zero STT Med cleared for PHI

The zero-med endpoint is the model path cleared for PHI. Non-PHI workloads can use zero-universal.

Audio retention

Audio is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256), deleted on transcription completion. Temp files purged within 24h.

On-prem data residency

For the strongest data-residency story, deploy on-prem. PHI never leaves the hospital network.
