BFSI — banking, financial services, insurance

BFSI does not have a dedicated vertical page on Shunya Labs' public site, but several capabilities in the catalog are oriented toward finance use cases — most notably the zero-numerical ASR model. This page lists the Shunya pieces you're most likely to reach for in a BFSI build.

Shunya capabilities most often used here

CapabilityShunya componentSource
Digit-heavy ASR (amounts, account numbers, dates) zero-numerical — listed on the Shunya pricing page Shunya pricing
Indian-language ASR for collections / customer calls zero-indic for general Indian-language audio; zero-codeswitch for Hinglish / Tanglish ASR API guide
Telephony I/O ulaw / alaw at 8 kHz on both ASR streaming and TTS — Twilio and most Indian PSTN systems use mu-law ASR + TTS docs
