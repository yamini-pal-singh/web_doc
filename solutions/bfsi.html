Common workflows Illustrative

EMI reminder & collection

  • Outbound call in customer's language via Twilio / Exotel.
  • Zero STT Indic transcribes the customer response streaming; zero-numerical for precise amount handling.
  • Intent detection classifies: will_pay, dispute, callback_later, cannot_pay.
  • Sentiment flags frustration for human escalation.
  • Zero TTS synthesises the next response in the customer's dialect with a consistent brand voice.
  • Hash keywords redacts account and card numbers from stored transcripts.

KYC voice verification

  • Customer speaks a challenge phrase; speaker identification matches it against their registered voiceprint.
  • Zero STT validates the phrase content. Combined score = biometric × semantic.
  • Stored as an immutable voice audit trail, redacted for PII.

Call QA and compliance

  • Full-call batch transcription with diarization.
  • Mandatory disclosures detected via keyterm normalization.
  • Automated QA scoring — did the agent follow the script? Did they use prohibited language?
  • Transcripts retained per RBI / SEBI retention guidelines in an on-prem store.

Compliance fit

RBI data localization

On-prem deployment keeps customer PII and audio inside India. Same container parity as the cloud service.

Audit trail

Every transcript carries request_id, timestamp, diarization, and intelligence outputs. Retained per regulator rules.

SEBI / IRDAI scripts

Mandatory disclosure detection ensures required language was spoken in full; failures flagged to supervisors.

PII redaction

Account numbers, cards, OTPs masked in stored text. Reduces blast radius of a data leak.
